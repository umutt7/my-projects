Autocompleter

HOW TO USE: As we mentioned in previous page, the website will try to autocomplete, or guess, the word you are typing in.

For example, start typing "apple" (without quotes). As you type the letter a, the program will list you all the words starting with a. Because there's a large amount of words in the program's dictionary, getting the results for the first few letters might take a long time, please be patient.

Mind that you do not have to hit enter the submit the letters you typed in. The program always watching what you've typed in.
